OUR FEATURED SPEAKERS

Our Speakers are passionate and driven change agents in their field of expertise. We hope their vision, experiences and unconventional approach will inspire more conversation and action on change.

Kapila Rasnayake

Kapila is a founder of Voices of Humans and works to promote a ‘Genderless society’. He is a global advocate on Gender, Human rights and sexuality. Kapila is an experienced community social worker specialising in Rights based Advocacy and working with marginalized groups. Kapila is a expert in art based approaches to address social Issues. He is also a playwright and a director. He has been engaged in youth activism for more than ten years with number of local and international organizations including the United Nations.

Lonali Rodrigo

Lonali Rodrigo is the founder CEO and lead designer behind House of Lonali the social enterprise that runs the ethical brand 'Lonali'. "Lonali" up-cycles apparel industry remnants minimizing the negative impact to the environment and maximizing the social benefit to the people through environmental friendly fashion and accessories produce in Sri Lanka.

Dhanika Perera

Dhanika Perera is an award-winning Serial Entrepreneur and the Founder of Bhasha, Helakuru and PayHere. He founded his first company Bhasha Lanka (Pvt) Ltd in 2011 as a Digital Technology company and his second company PayHere (Pvt) Ltd in 2016. He innovated a Sinhala Unicode rendering technology for Mobile devices as his university project and later commercialized it launching his first product "SETT Browser" as a solution to read Sinhala on Mobiles and then "Helakuru" as a solution to write Sinhala on Mobiles. Today Helakuru empowers millions of Sri Lankans to express themselves in Sinhala on Digital space & PayHere Today empowers thousands of Sri Lankan businesses to accept digital payments conveniently, accomplishing his personal vision to "Empower the Masses".
