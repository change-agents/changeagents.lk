ABOUT US

We identify the term Change Agents with individuals who are not only passionate and fearless but those who are using their potential to create positive impact. Change Agents is a new initiative created by AIESEC Alumni Sri Lanka. With its vision of “Peace and fulfilment of humankind’s potential”, AIESEC instils and inspires a philosophy of positive change in its stakeholders.

Change Agents will provide a platform to connect like-minded people in their pursuit of change for the betterment of our society. Our platform will be engaging thought leaders, fearless ideologies, passionate voices and aspiring visionaries.

Change Agents will not only feature presentations by notable speakers but will also provide spaces for audience participants to interact and converse around their subject of interest. Through these shared experiences we hope to create a meeting hub for experience sharing through which to drive a dialogue for change. Ultimately we hope to inspire, encourage and enable all those who are working towards positive change in our world today.

Through Change Agents we hope to…

  • Organise events which will provide a physical and virtual platform for Change Agents to share their experiences and expertise
  • Create a meeting hub to enable like-minded individuals to meet and drive ideas for change
  • Encourage a healthy dialogue on creating positive impact
  • Inspire more people to play an active role in bringing positive change to fruition
