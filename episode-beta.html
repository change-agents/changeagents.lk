We present to you Change Agents - Episode β!

In our second public event, we focus on delving in deeper to entrepreneurship and personal agency in creating change through the stories of three individuals from the fields of technology, fashion and gender equality.

Change Agents - Episode β is scheduled to take place on the 11th of May 2019 at Hatch from 3.00pm - 6.00pm. If you would like to attend, please click here to register for your free event pass. Limited seats available!

Change Agents - Episode β will offer an interactive space for the participants as well a continuing dialogue on the topics featured at the event. The event features three ground breaking individuals from the fields of technology, fashion and human rights. We will hear from Dhanika Perera, the Founder of Bhasha, Helakuru and Payhere, Lonali Rodrigo, the Founder CEO and Lead Designer at House of Lonali and Kapila Rasnayake, the founder of Voices of Human who and an expert on rights based advocacy.

This event is brought to you by AIESEC Alumni Sri Lanka and Hatch.

Change Agents Episode β is scheduled for the 11th of May 2019 at Hatch, No. 14, Sir Baron Jayathilaka Mawatha, Colombo 01 from 3.00pm - 6.00pm.
