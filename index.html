ABOUT US

Change Agents is a new initiative created by AIESEC Alumni Sri Lanka in order to encourage and inspire change in our society. Change Agents will provide a platform to connect like-minded people in their pursuit of change for the betterment of our society. Our platform will be engaging thought leaders, fearless ideologies, passionate voices and aspiring visionaries.

Change Agents will not only feature presentations by notable speakers but will also provide spaces for audience participants to interact and converse around their subject of interest. Through these shared experiences we hope to create a meeting hub for experience sharing through which to drive a dialogue for change.
