Education in the times of uncertainty Maria Lucia Uribe

Parenting with Metta Sifaan Zavahir

Parenting and education are two areas that are imperative in its contribution to the development of a socially responsible and compassionate human being. In our inaugural event, we focused on the importance of education and parenting and how we can contribute to the recalibration and a new vision for parenting and education.

Maria Lucia Uribe shared her thoughts on “Education in Times of Uncertainty” and Sifaan Zavahir shared his experience in “Parenting with Metta”.


Change Agents Episode α took place on the 26th of February 2018 at British Council, Orion City.
